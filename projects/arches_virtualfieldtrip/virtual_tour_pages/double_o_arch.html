Double O Arch

 

The Park is located in southeast Utah. It is high in elevation, between 4,000 and 5,600 feet above sea level. The area is sometimes referred to as a “cold” desert. Winters are cool, with low temperatures sometimes dropping below zero degrees Fahrenheit. Strong winds are common in spring. Summer temperatures often exceed 100 degrees Fahrenheit in July and August. A summer downpour can produce spectacular fl ash floods and water falls. Rainfall averages only ten inches per year.
