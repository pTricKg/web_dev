Delicate Arch

 

Some animals have adaptations that help them live in a dry, hot and cold desert. For instance,
black-tailed jackrabbit, kit fox, and mule deer all have large ears with lots of blood vessels in them. Â 
They stay cooler by radiating heat from their blood to the air. Some animals such as the kangaroo rat have specialized kidneys that can make water out of dry foods, and have specialized nasal passages that prevent too much moisture from escaping when they breathe.
